# 📊 Superstore Sales & Profit Analysis

## 🔍 Overview

This project analyzes Superstore retail data to understand why increasing sales are not translating into proportional profit. The focus is on identifying key drivers of margin decline and uncovering loss-making areas.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze sales and profit trends
* Identify key factors affecting profitability
* Detect loss-making products, regions, and segments
* Provide actionable business recommendations

---

## 🧠 Key Insights

* Discounts above 20% lead to significant profit loss
* Furniture category, especially Tables, is highly unprofitable
* High shipping costs reduce overall margins
* Certain regions consistently generate losses
* Sales growth does not guarantee profit growth

---

## 🔬 Root Cause

Profit decline is driven by a combination of:

* High discounting on low-margin products
* High shipping costs
* Unfavorable product mix
* Regional inefficiencies

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies

* **SQL** (Window Functions – LAG, LEAD)
* **Power BI** (Dashboard & Visualization)
* **Python** (Data Analysis)

---

## 📊 Key Features

* Sales vs Profit trend analysis
* Profit margin analysis
* Discount impact analysis
* Category and region performance breakdown
* Multi-dimensional analysis (Product × Region × Discount)

---

## 💡 Recommendations

* Optimize discount strategy
* Reduce or eliminate low-margin products
* Improve logistics to control shipping costs
* Focus on high-margin categories and regions

---

## 📌 Conclusion

The business shows strong sales performance but weak profitability. Optimizing pricing and cost strategies can significantly improve margins.
